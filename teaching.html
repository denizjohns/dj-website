Teaching

Deniz has extensive teaching experience at all levels of higher education in the UK, from undergraduate to PhD level. Her teaching experience covers a wide spectrum of subjects in theory and practice, both within her area of expertise and on broader discipline-relevant topics. Some of the titles of the courses she teaches/taught:

  • Critical reflections in Creative Art
  • Experimental Cinema: Theory and Practice
  • Avant-garde Film and Experimental Video
  • Documentary Film Theory and History
  • Documentary Film Practice
  • Transgressive Cinema: Advanced Practices
  • Video Production
  • Research Methods in Contemporary Film Theory
